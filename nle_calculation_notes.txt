In IBIS step we've added a counter on number of reweight calculations, a counter on the number of move triggers, and a counter on the number of likelihood evals during moves.
This is all within IBIS, so we will capture anything that happens within an IBIS step from this. 
IBIS is called with CSMC, but there are additional likelihood evals that happen in CSMC...
These are due to the reweighting of trajectories themselves. These have now been added to the count as nb_traj * nb_part evals and nb_traj calls.
